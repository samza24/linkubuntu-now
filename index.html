The problem

Every day people die or are critically injured in SA road accidents. Families wait hours, days, sometimes years to find out what happened. Bodies are buried as paupers because no one could identify them.
The current system fails because it depends on one outdated phone number. When that number is dead — the family connection is completely broken.

How LinkUbuntu works

1

Citizens build their emergency web

Register with your SA ID number. Add the cell numbers of everyone who needs to know if something happens to you. Update anytime — your web stays current.

2

Responder scans at the scene

SAPS officer, paramedic or traffic officer uses the responder app. The device's built-in fingerprint sensor identifies the victim from the database instantly.

3

Family notified in minutes

Real SMS sent to all emergency contacts in priority order. Responder can also call any contact directly from the app. Family knows within minutes — not days or years.
